How it works

From an hour of talk to a week of momentum.

Three things happen between the moment your meeting ends and the moment your team gets back to work.

01 · Distill

Cuts the noise.

Cadence reads the rambling transcript and pulls out only what matters — decisions, risks, open questions, and follow-ups. The rest gets archived, not lost.

~92% of notes flagged as talk-only
02 · Assign

Owns the follow-through.

Every action item lands on a real person with a real deadline, inferred from who said what. No more "we should" or "someone needs to." Just owners.

0 orphaned tasks, guaranteed
03 · Sync

Lives where you work.

Two-way sync with Linear, Jira, Asana, Notion, and Slack. Status changes flow back into Cadence, so your meeting recap is never out of date.
