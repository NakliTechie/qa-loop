§ 01 — About

Self-portrait Bergen · 2023

Photographs made slowly, on film, mostly in bad weather.

Elias Marén (b. 1986, Oslo) is a photographer working along the North Atlantic coast. His ongoing project Still Water documents the slow erosion of fishing communities from Lofoten to the Outer Hebrides — six years of medium-format film, bad ferries, and rented rooms above fish-processing plants.

Solo exhibitions include Foam Amsterdam (2022), Nordiska Museet Stockholm (2023), and Gallery Axel Oslo (2024). His first monograph was published by Mack Books in spring 2024; a second is in preparation.

Represented by Gallery Axel. Available for editorial and commission work across Scandinavia and the UK.
