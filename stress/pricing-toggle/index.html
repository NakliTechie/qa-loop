FAQ

Questions, answered.

Can I change plans or cancel anytime?

Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from your billing dashboard at any time. Changes prorate automatically, and we'll never charge a cancellation fee. If you cancel mid-cycle, you keep access until the period you've paid for ends.

How does the 14-day free trial work?

Every plan starts with a full-featured 14-day trial — no credit card required. At the end of the trial, you pick the plan that fits. If you do nothing, your workspace simply pauses until you choose.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or education?

We offer 40% off all plans for verified non-profits, accredited educational institutions, and students. Reach out to our team with your documentation and we'll apply the discount to your account within one business day.

What happens if I exceed my storage or seat limit?

We'll notify you by email before you hit your limit. You can either upgrade to the next tier or purchase additional storage and seats as add-ons. We never throttle or block your work without warning.

Is my data secure, and who owns it?

You always own your content. We're SOC 2 Type II compliant, encrypt data at rest and in transit, and run daily backups across three regions. You can export everything as a standard archive at any time from your dashboard.

Do you support custom contracts and invoicing?

For teams of 25 or more, we offer annual invoicing, custom MSAs, and dedicated procurement support. Contact our sales team and we'll work with your finance and legal departments to get everything in place.
