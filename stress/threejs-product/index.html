03 — In detail

Three things we argued about.

A short list of decisions that took longer than they should have.
i. The joint

Friction without springs.

A tapered brass collet sets the hold with a single set-screw. Adjust it once with a 2mm hex and the arm keeps its pose for a decade — no droop, no spring fatigue.

ii. The reflector

Spun, then hand-finished.

The shade is spun from a single disc of brass and linished by hand. The interior is left uncoated so it oxidises slowly — the pool of warm light deepens over years, not days.

iii. The base

Weight where it earns it.

Cast zinc, 1.1kg of the lamp's 2.4kg sits in the base. The driver lives there too, keeping the head cool and the shade small. Felt underside, because desks deserve it.
