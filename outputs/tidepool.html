Cupping notes

The Tidepool house blend, broken down.

We dial in every blend on a Probat L12 drum roaster and cup it blind against the previous week's batch. Below is the sensory profile of our current house blend — scored on a 10-point scale across four axes.

Acidity — 6/10
Bright apple-skin acidity that lifts the cup without turning sharp.
Body — 8/10
Syrupy, coating mouthfeel — holds up to milk without disappearing.
Sweetness — 7/10
Brown sugar and dried fig. No bitterness chasing it down.
Aroma — 9/10
Toasted walnut, cocoa nib, and a wisp of sea air from the coast.
